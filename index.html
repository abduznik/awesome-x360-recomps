Awesome Xbox 360 Recomps

A curated list of Xbox 360 game recompilation projects that bring classic titles to modern platforms natively — no emulation needed.

awesome
What is a recompilation? Static recompilation translates the original Xbox 360 PowerPC machine code into native C++ that runs directly on your hardware. The result is a native PC build with better performance, higher resolutions, ultrawide support, and mod capabilities — all without an emulator.

Skate 3

Native recompilation of Skate 3 for Windows, Linux, and macOS — runs at 4K 165fps with ultrawide support.

by mchughalex

Naughty Bear

PC port of Naughty Bear Gold Edition (all DLC included) for Windows.

by MaxDeadBear

Lollipop Chainsaw

PC port of Lollipop Chainsaw for Windows.

by MaxDeadBear

Banjo-Kazooie: Nuts & Bolts

Recompilation of Banjo-Kazooie: Nuts & Bolts for Windows and Linux, runnable via the Goopie launcher.

by masterspike52

Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise

Early-stage recompilation of Viva Piñata: Trouble in Paradise with mod support via function hooking, targeting Windows (Linux planned).

by SolarCookies
